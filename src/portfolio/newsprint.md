A newspaper-inspired blog theme that brings the classic publication experience to the web. Newsprint combines traditional typography and layout with modern web standards for serious publishers.

Key features:
- **Newsletter-first design**—built for email delivery and RSS feeds
- **Traditional newspaper aesthetics**—masthead, multi-column layouts, pull quotes
- **Category-specific RSS feeds**—News, Opinion, Features, Culture, Business
- **Rich typography**—drop caps, serif fonts, ruled lines, sepia palette
- **Print-optimized styles**—looks great when printed
- **Monetization ready**—built-in support for Ko-fi, Patreon, donations
- **Flexible front matter**—featured articles, excerpts, categories, authors

Perfect for serious publications, newsletters, and writers who want a professional reading experience.
